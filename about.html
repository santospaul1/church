Jesus Power Evangelism church

HISTORY

The church was founded by Dr Bishop Mzungu Chai. Its was founded in the year 2018. The church is located at Uasin Gishu County at Eldoret. The ministry has other anointed Men and Women of God serving together with residing Bishop.

Mission

Our mission is to glorify God through worship, serve our community, and make disciples of Jesus Christ.

Vision

Our vision is to be a welcoming and inclusive church, where people from all backgrounds find hope, healing, and a deep connection with God.

Core Values

Our core values include love, faith, community, and outreach.

Goals and Objective

Over the next five years, we aim to increase our membership by 20% and establish a new outreach program to support families in need.

Ministries and Programs

We offer a range of ministries, including youth groups, community service projects, and worship services that cater to all ages and backgrounds
